##### do + need
- I need ~ => ~ 가 필요하다.
- Do you need ~ ? => ~ 가 필요한가요?

#####  don't
- I don't need help. 

##### do + have
-  Do you have ~ ?
- Do you have clothes?
- Do you have time?
- I don't have time.

#####  do + look
- Does he look ~ ? => 그가 ~ 처럼 보이나요?
- Does he look scary? => 그가 무서워 보이나요?
-  Does she look good? => 그녀가 좋아 보이나요?

##### do you have?
- Do you have a package?
- Do you have an idea?
